§ 01  ·  Why now

AI is moving faster
than any business
can keep up with.

Most companies know AI matters. Few know how to put it to work in a way that holds up. The gap between knowing and doing widens every month.

Compound Labs is a consultancy with operating roots. We've run the systems AI depends on — finance, technology, people, product, sales — and built AI on top of them. Useful on the whole problem, not only the model.

§ 02  ·  How we work

AI compounds
what already works.

† The unglamorous part, done first — people, finances, systems.

A lot of people are promising miraculous results from AI right now. Very few of them have ever run the systems it depends on — technical, financial, social.

We have. We built and ran those systems inside real companies long before AI was the headline. That experience is the difference, because AI doesn't produce outsized results on top of systems that don't work. It accelerates whatever is already there, including the cracks.

So we work foundations-first — including strategy and operations consulting in its own right, no AI required. We make sure the systems hold, then apply AI where it can genuinely compound. That's the work that keeps paying back after we've left.

They'll be the ones built around it.
