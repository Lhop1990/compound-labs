§ 01  ·  Strategy

Strategy & Operations

AI inside the way the business actually runs.

For executive teams looking at AI through an operations lens. Where it fits in the org, what it changes about how teams work, and what to invest in first. Strategy that survives contact with reality.

We map the opportunity space against your operational realities: your data, your team, your customers. Then we produce a prioritised roadmap that ties AI investment to outcomes you can measure. No 50-slide decks. A working plan, fit for the room you'll defend it in.

What's included
  • Opportunity mapping across the business
  • Capability & data-readiness assessment
  • Prioritisation framework with scoring
  • Operating-model recommendations
  • Investment roadmap with sequencing
Engagement
Duration
4–8 weeks
Pricing
Fixed fee
Output
Roadmap & brief
Best when

You're at the strategic decision point. What to invest in, when, and through whom.

§ 02  ·  Build

Product Development

AI-native products, shipped. Not bolted on.

For businesses building software where AI is the core capability. Not a feature added late. We work end-to-end: from concept and architecture through evaluation framework and shipped MVP. The output is a working product, not a prototype.

Our team has built and shipped AI-first products at zero-to-one and at scale. We bring senior product, engineering, and AI judgement to the room, and leave you with a system your team owns and can extend without us.

What's included
  • Product discovery & user research
  • Technical architecture & AI system design
  • MVP build to production-grade
  • Evaluation & quality framework
  • Launch support & team handoff
Engagement
Duration
8–16 weeks
Pricing
Fixed phases
Output
Shipped product
Best when

You're building something AI-first and want senior people who've shipped it before.

§ 03  ·  Build

Agent Development

Working AI agents. Scoped, built, handed over.

We design and build AI agents that take on real operational work. Research, drafting, analysis, internal ops, support edge cases. Not chatbots. Not demos. Production-grade agents engineered for a specific job, with the evaluation harness to prove they're doing it well.

Every agent is scoped to a job description, owned end-to-end, and shipped with documentation that lets your team take over. We don't build dependency.

What's included
  • Job-description scoping & success criteria
  • Agent architecture & system design
  • Build, integration & deployment
  • Evaluation harness & monitoring
  • Documentation & full handoff
Engagement
Duration
4–8 weeks
Pricing
Fixed fee
Output
Live agent
Best when

You've identified a repeatable, high-friction task or role that would benefit from being handled by an agent.

§ 04  ·  Tactical

AI Automations

Targeted automation that removes operational friction.

Smaller in scope than full agents. Focused on the specific bottleneck eating hours every week. We audit the workflow, design the automation, build it on the right stack (Zapier, Make, n8n, custom code, or a blend), and hand it over with monitoring in place.

Most of these engagements pay for themselves inside a quarter.

What's included
  • Workflow audit & ROI scoping
  • Integration build on the right stack
  • Model orchestration where useful
  • Monitoring & error handling
  • Maintenance plan & handoff
Engagement
Duration
2–4 weeks
Pricing
Fixed fee
Output
Live workflow
Best when

A high-volume, predictable task is eating real hours, and the workflow is well-defined enough to automate.

§ 05  ·  Adoption

AI Change Management

Adoption that actually sticks.

Most internal AI tools fail at adoption. Not at the technology. We help organisations roll out AI tooling internally by combining product, change, and training disciplines so the tools actually get used.

We design the rollout, identify and equip internal champions, build the training, and define the success metrics. What you've invested in becomes part of how the work gets done.

What's included
  • Adoption-readiness assessment
  • Rollout sequencing & comms plan
  • Training & enablement design
  • Internal champion programme
  • Success metrics & review cadence
Engagement
Duration
6–12 weeks
Pricing
Fixed phases
Output
Live programme
Best when

You've built or bought AI tooling and need it to land across the org. Not sit on a shelf.

§ 06  ·  Retainer

Ongoing Advisory

A senior thinking partner on retainer.

For executives making frequent AI-shaped decisions and lacking a senior in-house specialist. We sit alongside you as a thinking partner. Diligence on tools and vendors, sanity checks on your team's output, briefings on what just shipped and why it matters, and judgement calls when the path forward isn't obvious.

This is not consulting in the deck-producing sense. It's working access to a small set of people who've built and shipped this stuff.

What's included
  • Bi-weekly working sessions
  • Async access · Slack & email
  • Vendor & tool diligence
  • Industry briefings on what's shifting
  • Hiring & org-design input
Engagement
Duration
3–6 months
Pricing
Monthly retainer
Output
Continuous
Best when

You're making AI-shaped calls every week, and don't have (or aren't ready to hire) a senior specialist.

§ 07  ·  Embedded

Fractional AI Operations

Senior AI operations leadership. Embedded, fractionally.

For organisations that need senior AI operations capability now, but aren't ready (or shouldn't be) hiring full-time. We embed inside your team a few days a week, owning an active backlog of agents, automations, adoption work, and vendor decisions. Effectively part of your leadership.

This is the deepest engagement we offer. Continuity, not a project. You get senior judgement in the room, the work moving forward, and a steady hand on what AI is doing across the organisation week to week.

What's included
  • Embedded leadership · 1–3 days/week
  • Active project portfolio management
  • Vendor & tool diligence + procurement
  • Team coaching & capability building
  • Direct stakeholder & board engagement
Engagement
Duration
6–12 months
Pricing
Monthly retainer
Output
Continuous ops
Best when

You need senior AI ops capability now, and you want continuity over another project.
