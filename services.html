§ 01  ·  Strategy

Strategy & Operations

Where AI fits in your business — and how to get there.

For executive teams thinking about AI through an operations lens. Where it should fit in the organisation, what it changes about how teams work, and what to invest in first.

We map the opportunity space against your operational reality — your data, your team, your customers — and produce a prioritised roadmap that ties every investment to outcomes you can measure. A working plan, not a 50-slide deck.

Some of this work has nothing to do with AI. When the foundations need building first, we say so — and we do that work too. The AI layer comes when the systems can carry it.

What's included
  • Opportunity mapping across the business
  • Capability & data-readiness assessment
  • Prioritisation framework with scoring
  • Operating-model recommendations
  • Investment roadmap with sequencing

Agent Development

Working agents. Scoped, built, handed over.

We design and build agents that take on real operational work — research, drafting, analysis, internal ops, edge-case support. Production-grade agents engineered for a specific job, with the evaluation harness to prove they're doing it well.

Every agent is scoped to a job description, owned end-to-end, and handed over with the documentation your team needs to run it. We don't build dependency.

What's included
  • Job-description scoping & success criteria
  • Agent architecture & system design
  • Build, integration & deployment
  • Evaluation harness & monitoring
  • Documentation & full handoff

§ 04  ·  Tactical

AI Automations

Focused automation against specific bottlenecks.

Smaller in scope than a full agent. Aimed at the bottleneck that's eating hours every week. We audit the workflow, design the automation, build it on the right stack — Zapier, Make, n8n, custom code, or a blend — and hand it over with monitoring in place.

Every automation is scoped to a tangible recovery — time, cost, or risk — measured before we start and again once it's live.

What's included
  • Workflow audit & ROI scoping
  • Integration build on the right stack
  • Model orchestration where useful
  • Monitoring & error handling
  • Maintenance plan & handoff

§ 05  ·  Adoption

AI Change Management

Adoption that sticks.

Most internal AI tools fail at adoption, not at the technology. We help organisations roll them out properly — combining product, change, and training so the tools actually get used.

We design the rollout, identify and equip internal champions, build the training, and define the success metrics. What you've invested in becomes part of how the work gets done day to day.

What's included
  • Adoption-readiness assessment
  • Rollout sequencing & comms plan
  • Training & enablement design
  • Internal champion programme
  • Success metrics & review cadence

A senior partner on retainer.

For executives making AI-shaped decisions every week without a senior specialist in-house. Working access to people who've built and shipped this stuff. Not consulting in the deck-producing sense.

  • Industry briefings on what's shifting
  • Hiring & org-design input
